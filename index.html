About Batak Toba Script

The Batak Toba script, known as Surat Batak or Si Sia-sia, is an ancient writing system used by the Batak Toba people of North Sumatra, Indonesia.

This beautiful script has been used for centuries to record religious texts, genealogies, and traditional literature. It is an abugida writing system, where each consonant has an inherent vowel that can be modified with diacritical marks.

Today, preserving and promoting the Batak Toba script is essential for maintaining the cultural heritage of the Batak people. Write Toba aims to make learning this script accessible and enjoyable for everyone.
